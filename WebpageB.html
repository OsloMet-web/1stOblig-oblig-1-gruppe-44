Our Old Highschool

Here's some noteworthy parts of our schools.

Gaaviga

My highschool was a three floour private school for students who did sports. Students there had study specialization with their sport they were doing. The canteen had good food with saladbar and big portion of dinners, but it was a bit expensive so we often went to the rema nearby our school. We also had a big trainingstudio in the basement that had cycles, treadmill, exercise equipment and weights.

Jenny

I went to a high school that had different buildings for specific programme subjects. The main building was mainly used for the common core subjects and had a sizable cafeteria. My building was not as up-to-date as the main building, but still had the proper equipment for the programme subject I went for.

Oda

At my old high school the sports hall where we had our PE classes was bigger and more modern than the main teaching building. In the main teaching building all the doors on the ground floor was painted red, the ones on the first floor was blue, and all the doors at the top floor was painted yellow. The newest building on the school was built over thirty years ago.
